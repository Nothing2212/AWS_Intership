### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. FCJ members are always willing to help whenever I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is tidy and comfortable, helping me focus better. However, I think it would be nice to have more social gatherings or team bonding activities to strengthen relationships.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides very detailed guidance, explains clearly when I don't understand, and always encourages me to ask questions. The admin team supports administrative tasks, provides necessary documents, and creates favorable conditions for me to work effectively. I especially appreciate that the mentor allows me to try and solve problems myself instead of just giving the answer.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks I was assigned align well with the knowledge I learned at university, while also introducing me to new areas I had never encountered before, especially when moving from a Software Engineer role into DevOps. This allowed me to both strengthen my foundational knowledge and gain practical skills in deploying and operating systems.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, professional communication in a corporate environment, and getting started building a project with a serverless architecture. The mentor also shared valuable real-world experiences that helped me better plan my career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, works seriously but still keeps things enjoyable. When there are urgent projects, everyone works together and supports one another regardless of their position. This made me feel like a real part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides an internship allowance and offers flexible working hours when needed. In addition, having the opportunity to join internal training sessions and knowledge-sharing events (such as Meeting 30/5 and Meeting 06/6) is a big plus.

### Additional Questions

- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
What I found most satisfying was getting to try different roles (moving from Software Engineer to DevOps), being trusted by my mentor with real work and supported whenever needed, and getting to attend meetings where colleagues shared valuable knowledge.

- **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
I think the company could hold more periodic review/feedback sessions during the internship so interns have a clearer picture of their progress and areas to improve, rather than only being evaluated at the end.

- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
Yes, I would recommend it because of the friendly environment, the chance to learn hands-on cloud/serverless technology, and mentors who are always ready to help interns grow both technically and in soft skills.

### Suggestions & Expectations

- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
I would suggest organizing more team bonding or cross-team social activities for interns, as well as providing clearer learning materials/roadmaps early on to help new interns get up to speed faster.

- **Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
Yes, I would like to keep contributing to FCJ's future projects if given the opportunity.

- **Any other comments (free sharing):**  
Thank you to the FCJ team and mentors for giving me a memorable internship that helped me grow both professionally and in my work attitude.
